About Mud and Miracles

Mud and Miracles is the young adult ministry of Calvary Chapel in Half Moon Bay, California.

Ryan and Nicole Petrik

Ryan and Nicole Petrik have served as missionaries in Cajamarca, Peru since 2011, sent out from Calvary Chapel Santa Cruz. What started as a simple plan to spend a couple of years there with their three (now, four) children has become a fruitful five year journey that God continues to bless. Ryan serves as the pastor of Refugio Iglesia Cristiana (Refuge Christian Church) in Cajamarca, works closely with El Refugio Orphanage & Children's Home, and recently started a skateboarding ministry with the local youth.

Calvary Chapel HMB have been longtime friends and supporters of the Petriks, and the Mud and Miracles group in particular has had a special connection with Ryan and Nicole. We wanted to do something special to support their ministry, so next April we will be travelling to Peru to serve in any and every way we can. Our group has a surprisingly diverse set of skills, and we are working closely with Ryan on identifying their greatest needs that we can help meet.
